Fruity Pebbles: The Game

Technologies used: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Howler.js

I was always curious how games worked, and had an idea for a simple and absurd game. So in 2014, I read a few books, and developed a very inefficient Fruity Pebbles: The Game. I rewrote it in 2016, and brought it to ZapCon, a local arcade convention. After that, it appeared on a podcast, YouTube, and even a top games of 2016 list.

Fruity Pebbles: The Game tells the story of what happens next after a Fruity Pebbles commercial. The game starts with the commercial. Because this is important to the plot, it can't be skipped:
